Deleting an account

To delete your account in our application, as well as all your data from our platform, you can click on the gear icon

then click on "Delete Account"

and click on "Delete Account" again, the latter is needed to confirm that you definitely want to delete your account with all notes and tasks

Unlinking Social Networks

If you want to unlink your social media accounts (such as Google or Facebook), you need to follow these steps:

For Google:

1) Sign in to your Google account.

2) Go to your security and privacy settings.

3) Find the "Connected apps and sites" section.

4) Find our app in the list of connected apps and uninstall it.

For Facebook:

1) Sign in to your Facebook account.

2) Go to "Settings" and select "Apps & websites".

3) Find our application in the list and uninstall it.

Deleting messages from social networks

We do not store messages received from your social media account. To delete messages, you must log into your social network account and delete them there.

Deleting notes

In our application, you can delete the notes you have created by simply clicking on the cross.

And then confirm the deletion by clicking on "Yes"
