User Rights

As a user of our website, you have certain rights in relation to your personal data. We are committed to providing transparency and control over your information.

Right to restriction of processing: In certain cases, you can request restriction of the processing of your data. For example, if you consider that the processing is unlawful or the data is no longer needed.


Right to data portability: You may request that your data be transferred to another service provider in a format that suits you, where technically feasible.


Right to Recovery: If a data breach occurs and your information becomes available to third parties, we will take all reasonable steps to restore the security of your data and notify you of the incident.


We make every effort to enforce your rights. If you would like to exercise your rights or have questions regarding the processing of your information, please contact us.


We value your privacy and will respect your rights under applicable law and our privacy policy.
